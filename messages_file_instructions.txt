Attendance Project Messaging Module Instructions

To get Message.java running you need to add mail.jar into the project library. Just follow the steps:

1. First download the "mail.jar" file.
2. On the project right click and go to "Properties".
3. In properties click on "Java Build Path".
4. Choose the "Libraries" tab.
5. Click "Add External JARs..."
6. choose the "mail.jar" where ever it is you saved it on your computer.
